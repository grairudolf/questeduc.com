Being a student can be alot to handle. The pressure of a new class, new classmates, new teachers and everything just seems to pressure us a lot. In life today some have no understanding of why we have to go to school because our parents don’t make us understand and our teachers are just there to make sure we cover the syllabus, this is one of major problems as a student. There is hardly any major difference we can make in our education if we don’t even know why in the hell we have a right to study in the first place. There are so many pressure that comes with being a student especially for those in underdeveloped areas who can barely have view of the wonders of knowledge. About 15% of the world have little or no knowledge and education in creating or adding beneficiaries to their society, they just work with what they are given and this is because they have little or no understanding of impact and success. They might even lack dreams. They only way to remove ignorance on the role of Education is to sensitize people of What it means to be educated, How to recieve this education, and What to do with the education you are to aquire along the way.
